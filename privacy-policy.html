Website Visitors

Long Story Studios's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Long Story Studios's visitors use its website. From time to time, Long Story Studios may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Long Story Studios also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on http://longstorystudios.com blog posts. Long Story Studios only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below.

Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, Long Story Studios uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Long Story Studios uses cookies to help Long Story Studios identify and track visitors, their usage of http://longstorystudios.com, and their website access preferences. Long Story Studios visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Long Story Studios's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Long Story Studios's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Long Story Studios's use of cookies.
