Q: What brought you to LS Studios? What's been your involvement so far?

A: I met Arren and Aiden at a VR-focused hackathon and ended up working with them. We ended up putting out a really good project and they ended up offering me a spot here at LS. Since then I’ve been working on the integration of a shop for Unit 108.

Q: What are you currently studying/working towards?

A: I am currently studying Mathematics-Computer Science at UCSD and am hoping to use this to get myself into the game dev industry.

Q: What would be a dream project for LS Studios?

A: A dream project for me would be either working on fully-immersive VR or just working with a team to make a game that I’d be really passionate about.

Q: What are some your favorite games/inspirations?

A: Some of my favorite games include Detroit: Become Human, Final Fantasy XV, the Dishonored series, Portal 2, Assassins Creed Rogue, and NieR: Automata. I love games that have a really good soundtrack or ones that just immerse me into the game's universe.
